About Us

Promoting unity, service, and good governance.

What we do

KaugnayPH is a web-based platform designed to improve communication between Barangay 761 and its residents. It allows users to stay updated with announcements, submit concerns, and access barangay services.

By offering neighborhood services and delivering important news and updates, Barangay 761 serves the community. By disseminating significant notifications and updates and offering local services,

These announcements serve for informing residents about programs, events, and other issues relevant to the barangay.

Mission & Vision

Mission

"To promote a community based on transparency, trust, and active involvement by offering easily available, effective, and responsive barangay services that protect the welfare, security, and dignity of each and every Barangay 761 resident."

"We are committed to delivering fair and timely assistance to all, ensuring no resident is left behind."

Vision

"A forward-thinking, unified Barangay 761 where all residents have access to a secure, healthy, and empowered community, supported by responsible governance and inclusive development for all."

"We envision a barangay where every voice is heard and every need is met with compassion and integrity."

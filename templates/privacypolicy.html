Privacy Policy

Learn how KaugnayPH collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

I. Overview

At KaugnayPH, we value the privacy of our residents and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

II. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from residents for the purpose of providing our services, including but not limited to:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Address
  • Date of Birth
  • Other information necessary for identity verification

III. How We Use Your Information

Your information is collected and used for the following purposes:

  • Verifying resident identity for access to our services
  • Processing requests, complaints, or document applications
  • Sending announcements or important notifications
  • Maintaining accurate records for barangay administration purposes

IV. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared only with authorized personnel or government entities when necessary to provide services or comply with legal requirements.

For quick answers to common concerns and inquiries, please use this feature to browse frequently asked questions.

SMS Alerts notify residents of important barangay announcements, events, and updates directly to their mobile phones.

You can register for SMS alerts by creating an account and enabling SMS notifications in your profile settings.

You can unsubscribe by going to your profile settings and toggling off the SMS subscription option.

KaugnayPH is a barangay management system that connects residents with their local barangay office for complaints, documents, and announcements.

Log in to your account, go to File a Complaint, fill out the form with the required details, and submit.

Go to Track Submissions in your account to view the current status of all your filed complaints and document requests.
